# Image Generation Prompt

## Core Subject

A mother taking her daughter to school on a motorcycle - capturing the warmth of daily routine and maternal care

## Scene Composition

- **Perspective:** Side view, 3/4 angle
- **Subjects Position:** Mother driving in front, daughter sitting behind holding mother's waist
- **Setting:** Morning scene, suburban street with school building silhouette in background
- **Mood:** Warm, caring, peaceful morning routine
- **Time of Day:** Early morning (soft golden hour lighting)

## Visual Style

- **Design Concept:** Modern minimalist illustration
- **Rendering Style:** Flat design with subtle gradients
- **Color Palette:**
  - Primary: Warm pastels (soft pink, peach, cream)
  - Accent: Teal/turquoise for contrast
  - Background: Light blue sky gradient
- **Visual Tone:** Vibrant yet harmonious, not overly saturated

## Technical Specifications

- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (Square)
- **Resolution:** 1024 x 1024 pixels
- **Format:** PNG with transparency support
- **Quality:** Print-ready, high resolution

- ✓ Only hands visible (if necessary for composition)
- ✓ Body language conveys emotion (since faces are omitted)
- ✓ Dignified, respectful portrayal

- **Motorcycle:** Modern automatic scooter, neutral color (white/silver)
- **Background:** Simplified school building, trees, minimal traffic
- **Focal Point:** The mother-daughter bond through posture and positioning
- **Visual Hierarchy:** Subjects in foreground (sharp), background (slightly blurred/simplified)
